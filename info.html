About

I started as an animator and multimedia artist — spending years learning how motion, composition, and visual storytelling communicate without words. That foundation changed how I think about product design entirely.

When I transitioned to UX, I brought that visual sensibility with me. I don't just solve usability problems — I think about how a product feels to use, how it communicates trust, and how each interaction moment shapes a person's perception of a brand.

Over four years I've shipped products used by 150K+ people across AI platforms, healthcare, and wellness. Currently wrapping up my MS in User Experience at Arizona State University (4.0 GPA), where I've been deepening my research practice alongside professional work.

I believe the best design disappears — it doesn't call attention to itself, it just makes the thing you're trying to do feel obvious. That's the standard I hold my work to.
